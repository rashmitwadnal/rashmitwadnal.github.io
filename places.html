Places in Solapur

Siddheshwar Temple

One of the most important spiritual landmarks located near Siddheshwar Lake.

Pandharpur

Located 70 km away, famous for Vitthal Rukmini Temple and Wari pilgrimage.

Akkalkot

40 km away, known for Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj temple.

Solapur Fort

A historic monument built during the Bahmani Sultanate period.
